# Getting Started

The fastest path from zero to a working MeshCore node. Follow these pages in
order to flash firmware, connect a companion app, and send your first message.

By the end of this section you will have:

- [ ] A supported LoRa device with MeshCore firmware flashed
- [ ] A client app connected to your device
- [ ] Sent (and received) your first mesh message

**Estimated time:** 15 minutes of reading + 5–10 minutes of flash time.

---

1. [What Is MeshCore?](what-is-meshcore.md) — the two-minute overview of what you're getting into
2. [What You Need](what-you-need.md) — hardware, apps, and regional frequency
3. [Flash Your First Device](flash-your-first-device.md) — firmware on device in a few clicks
4. [Connect a Client](connect-a-client.md) — pair the companion app and set your frequency
5. [Send Your First Message](send-your-first-message.md) — the five-minute win
